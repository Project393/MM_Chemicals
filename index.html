Our Product Range

⚗️ Chemicals

🔴
Chromic Acid
Used in hard chrome plating, surface treatment, and as an oxidising agent in electroplating baths.
🧂
Sodium Hypophosphite
Key reducing agent used in electroless nickel plating for uniform coating on complex geometries.
🔬
Boric Acid
Buffer agent widely used in nickel and tin plating baths to stabilise pH and improve deposit quality.
🟦
Copper Sulphate
Primary source of copper ions in acid copper electroplating baths for decorative and PCB applications.
🟩
Nickel Sulphate
Main nickel salt in Watts-type nickel electroplating solutions for bright and semi-bright deposits.
🌊
Nickel Chloride
Enhances anode dissolution and conductivity in nickel plating baths alongside nickel sulphate.
⬜
Barium Carbonate
Used to precipitate sulphate impurities in zinc cyanide plating baths, maintaining bath purity.
🟣
Potassium Permanganate
Powerful oxidising agent used in pre-treatment, cleaning, and etching processes in plating lines.
⚙️
CR 842 Additive
Speciality chrome plating additive for improved deposit brightness, coverage, and efficiency.
💎
Palladium Chloride
Used as an activator in electroless plating processes, especially for metallising non-conductive substrates.
